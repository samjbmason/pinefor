Pinefor is sunsetting

Pinefor was created as a side project to solve a problem about sharing gift lists. I thought I would have more time to spend on it than I actually did and therefore due to increasing time constraints and also a lack of time to market the product and generate some revenue, I have decided to sunset the project.

If people would like any of their data please email sam.jbmason at gmail dot com, it will be available for 2 months until 1st December 2014. After which I will be securely shutting down the servers an erasing them of any personal data.

If anyone is interested in picking up the project and taking it for themselves you can find all the source code (minus any user data, obviously!) on GitHub.

A big thanks to the people that used Pinefor and showed interest.
